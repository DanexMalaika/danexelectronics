About Danex Electronics

Founded in 2015, Danex Electronics has grown from a small electronics shop to become one of the leading technology retailers in the region. Our mission is to bring innovative technology solutions to everyday life, making advanced electronics accessible to everyone.

We specialize in the latest gadgets, computers, home entertainment systems, and smart home devices. Our team of technology experts is dedicated to providing exceptional customer service and expert advice to help you find the perfect tech solution.

Our Journey

2015 - Founded

Danex Electronics opens its first store in Silicon City, specializing in mobile devices and accessories.

2018 - Expansion

We expand our product range to include laptops, gaming systems, and home entertainment.

2020 - Online Store

Launch of our e-commerce platform, serving customers nationwide with fast delivery.

2023 - Innovation Hub

Establishment of our Technology Innovation Center, offering workshops and repair services.
